Our work

Awaj Foundation is committed and pledge bound to develop a universal platform for the workers, especially for women workers. The major objective of Awaj Foundation builds on welfare for all and endeavor of foundation is particularly devoted to undertake welfare activities for destitute and neglected women, children, elderly, disables and workers.

In the factory

We work in the factories to train workers and management members on legal rights, responsibilities and productive negotiations to improve working conditions.
