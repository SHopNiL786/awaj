Our offices

Awaj Foundation

5/F, (3rd Floor), Rsulbagh, Mohakhali
Dhaka – 1212, Bangladesh

In addition, Awaj operates 22 Women’s cafes in Dhaka, Gazipur, Ashulia, Savar, Narayanganj and Chittagong. The women’s cafes are centers where garment workers, particularly women, can come for services (such as legal aid, health), receive training, engage in recreational activities and develop connections with other workers.
